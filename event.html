EcoVerse


Type: Hackathon & Environmental Summit

About Event

EcoVerse is a unique event designed to bring together individuals from diverse fields to
tackle environmental challenges, with a primary focus on climate change.

The event will include a hackathon where participants develop innovative solutions for environmental
issues and a summit featuring keynote speakers and panel discussions on key topics such as
sustainable development, technology, AI, content creation, and entrepreneurship.

The event will aim to:
- Raise awareness about climate change and environmental conservation.
- Empower participants to apply their skills in fields like technology, AI, and entrepreneurship to address environmental challenges.
- Foster cross-disciplinary collaboration and networking for impactful environmental action.

Date: November 22, 2024

Location: Nile university
